Join our community where you'll be seen, heard, and valued.
Together, we create safer, more equitable spaces for women. With us, you'll belong.

Why This Is Needed

Style A — White

Education

Girls often mask challenges, appearing socially compliant while trying to cope internally, leading to overlooked learning needs. Our educators are able to upskill and develop organisations, reframe perspectives, allowing for a safer, more equitable space for women.

Health

Late recognition of neurodivergent women is usually down to misdiagnosis, confusing it with anxiety, depression, eating disorders, burnout and more. Hormonal transitions like menstruation, pregnancy, and menopause, intersect uniquely with neurodivergence but remain under-researched. Our clinicians specialise in assessing neurodivergent women. Your health is safer with us.

Work & Society

Women on the Spectrum who are undiagnosed or late-diagnosed lead to compounded employment challenges, missed career opportunities and even leading to unemployment. Our mentoring and seminar sessions aim to provide women with a fairer chance at succeeding at work.

Social

Women are more likely to be misunderstood, mislabelled, or under-supported, often leading to loneliness. Our social events & peer networking sessions bring women on the spectrum together, like-minded people building relationships that last!

Why This Is Needed

Style A — White

Social Life

Our social events & peer networking sessions bring women on the spectrum together, like-minded people building relationships that last!

Health

Our clinicians specialise in assessing and treating neurodivergent women. Late recognition of neurodivergent women is usually down to misdiagnosis, confusing it with anxiety, depression, eating disorders, burnout and more. Hormonal transitions like menstruation, pregnancy, and menopause, intersect uniquely with neurodivergence but remain under-researched.

Your health is safer with us.

Work

Women on the Spectrum who are undiagnosed or had a late-diagnosis, often face compounded challenges at work. Our mentoring, close group seminars and career-oriented events are here to ensure women on the spectrum feel comfortable and confident operating effectively in these working spaces.

Training

Girls often mask challenges, appearing socially compliant while trying to cope internally, leading to overlooked needs, in healthcare, learning in school and even in the workplace. Our educators are able to upskill and develop organisations, while reframing their perspectives, allowing for safer and more equitable spaces for women on the spectrum.

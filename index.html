The Reality

Why This Is Needed

Systemic gaps that demand urgent, compassionate action

Style A — Light Cards
📚

Education

Girls are diagnosed later or not at all — leaving them unsupported through critical developmental years with no tailored learning strategies.

🩺

Health

Autistic women face compounded health inequities — from misdiagnosis to inadequate mental health support, often exhausted by masking.

💼

Work & Society

Neurodivergent women are underemployed and undervalued — despite exceptional skills, they face bias that excludes them from meaningful careers.

🌸

Social

Isolation, burnout, and a profound lack of community leave neurodivergent women feeling invisible — in a world built without them in mind.
